Color Game

Instructions

Drag the circles into the squares to change their color. You win when the two colors match. Yry to do it in as few moves as possible!

The colors combine simply by averageing each of the red, blue and green values. For example, a bright red mixed with a bright blue will give an output of medium red+medium blue, or a dull purple.

Try to be creative! While you can simply drag the most similar color to the oposite square, you can often get a solution in fewer turns by analyzing the colors carefully.
