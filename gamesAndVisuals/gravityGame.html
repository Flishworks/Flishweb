Orbital

Instructions

Launch satellites into orbit! Click and hold the mouse to build up power, move the mouse to aim your shot, and release to launch a satellite. You score every time a satellite orbits the center planet. The goal is to get the highest score you can in one minute.

You lose (and record no score) if a satellite crashes into the center planet. Satellites will not crash into each other. You can launch as many satellites as you want, but be careful, their gravity will affect the orbit of each other.
